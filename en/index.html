nce, in another world that still resembled ancient Finland, there was an immense enchanted forest called Hämärä. In its depths lived mystical beings, from tiny sprites to elusive guardians, water spirits, giants, and divine entities like Ahti, Tapio, and Louhi.

Hämäränkävijät is an adventure roleplaying game for the whole family (3+), where players resolve conflicts between people and mythological beings.

The game takes place in a Kalevala-inspired fantasy world where heroes can be gentle wanderers, calm healers, clever sages, skilled smiths, or strong protectors.

Players roll one to five dice depending on their hero's strengths. Heroes can also use Luck, Courage, and special Abilities to shape the story.

What is roleplaying?

Roleplaying is a form between game and play where participants build a shared story. The goal is not to defeat other players, but to describe what each hero does in the world. One participant is the narrator who presents the world and challenges.
