Hämäränkävijät was born from Roosa Melkko's roleplaying sessions with her young child. Many existing roleplaying systems focus on overcoming enemies, but children often seek friendship, empathy, and peaceful outcomes in stories.

That observation inspired a game designed from the ground up for non-violent conflict resolution, emotional literacy, and collaborative imagination. The structure is intentionally approachable and does not require reading-heavy rules.

The long-term vision is to make tabletop roleplaying accessible to families, schools, and youth groups regardless of previous RPG experience.
